About PES UNIVERSITY:

PES University in Bangalore, India, focuses on teaching students practical skills for a changing world. It was ranked fifth by Outlook-ICARE India in 2020, with key grading criteria like FSR and Inclusiveness & Diversity. PES University is #1 Private University in Karnataka for Management Education. The institution emphasizes values and skills, with faculty leading the way in delivering top educational programs. Students enjoy a vibrant campus life with excellent infrastructure support.

Event Rules and Guidelines

  • Rule 1: The competition is open to currently enrolled college students. Teams may consist of individuals or a maximum number of participants per team (e.g., 3-5 members)..
  • Rule 2: Teams must register within the specified deadline. Provide necessary information, including team members' names, contact details, and academic affiliations..
  • Rule 3: Submissions must be original ideas or innovative solutions to real-world business challenges.
  • Rule 4: Participants can work on developing a new product, service, or improving an existing concept Ideas must align with the competition's theme or problem statements.
    • Rule 5: Attendance at relevant workshops is encouraged to enhance understanding of critical concepts such as value proposition, market validation, financial projections, and go-to-market strategy.
  • Rule 6: Follow the specified report format for submission, including proposed product/service, target market, competitive landscape, plan for execution, revenue model, and financial projections.
  • Rule 7: Clearly communicate the deadline for the submission of reports and any additional materials..
  • Rule 8: Reports and presentations will be evaluated based on various criteria, including market analysis, financial projections, competitive advantage, and the viability of the venture.
  • Rule 9: The competition may involve multiple rounds, such as idea finalization, business canvas creation, and pitching to venture capitalists.
